Behind the Flavor

Charred Fork is a bold, contemporary fast-food brand that redefines comfort food with a rustic twist. Specializing in rotisserie chicken, wraps, pizzas, and thirst-quenching beverages, Charred Fork blends the raw essence of flame-grilled flavor with a sharp, modern personality.

Master Chef Thyme

With over 20 years of experience in both traditional cuisine and modern gastronomy, Chef Thyme creates dishes that honor the past while embracing the future. Each plate tells a story of cultural fusion and culinary excellence.
